Destiny

Q: How can I help Destiny become a better product?

A: We love getting kudos, but getting a pat on the back only goes so far. Tell us what's wrong and we'll get to work. Log bugs here or tell us how we can make the app better by adding a feature request here.

Feature Requests

Q: How do feature requests get implemented?

A: Feature requests are worked on based on the results of the "Features Voting Poll". Users, like you, can donate towards specific features to get implemented. Once the donation goal is achieved, then our engineers will get to work on that feature.

Q: How do I donate towards a feature?

A: Go to the Features ticket page here and click on the in the "Donate" column. From there, you'be be guided through a donations form.

Note: You'll need to have a credit card on file before donating towards a feature. You can add one in your profile, here

Bugs

Q: What's the difference between voting for bugs and voting for feature requests?

A: The main difference between voting for bugs and voting for features is that donations are not needed to get bugs fixed. Users have already donated towards the feature. We've implemented it. Now, there's a bug in the feature. Users shouldn't have to pay double to fix our mistakes. Voting for a bug let's us know which bugs to work on ASAP.

Q: Wait, but all bugs should be fixed ASAP. Why aren't you working on all of them?

A: We agree! We want to fix all our bugs as quickly as possible. Our engineering team is always finding more efficient processes and solutions to keep our customers happy.
