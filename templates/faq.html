General Questions

What does Quo Vadimus mean?

It's Latin. It means "Where are we going?"

Are all the companies and employees real?

Of course not, but i think now it's a good time to acknowledge where these come from:

Quo Vadimus is the name of the company the character Calvin Trager founded in the show, "Sports Night".

Pied Piper and Hooli are from the amazing HBO show, "Silicon Valley". Check it out!

Wayne Enterprises is from the Batman and DC franchises.

Stark Industries is from the Iron Man and Marvel franchises.

Dunder Mifflin is the ficticious company from the NBC show, "The Office".

Entertainment 720 is a hilariously failed company from the NBC show, "Parks and Recreation".

Feature Request Tickets

How do feature requests get implemented?

Feature requests are worked on based off of the results of the Features Voting Poll. Users, like you, can donate towards specific features to get implemented. Once the donation goal is achieved, then our engineers will get to work on that feature.

How do I donate towards a feature?

Go to the Features ticket page here and click on the in the "Donate" column. From there, you'be be guided through a donations form.

Note: You'll need to have a credit card on file before donating towards a feature. You can add one in your profile, here

Bug Tickets

What's the difference between voting for bugs and voting for feature requests?

The main difference between voting for bugs and voting for features is that donations are not needed to get bugs fixed. Users have already donated towards the feature. We've implemented it. Now, there's a bug in the feature. Users shouldn't have to pay double to fix our mistakes. Voting for a bug let's us know which bugs to work on ASAP.

Wait, but all bugs should be fixed ASAP. Why aren't you working on all of them?

We agree! We want to fix all our bugs as quickly as possible. Our engineering team is always finding more efficient processes and solutions to keep our customers happy.
